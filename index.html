Sasha Njini

creative + enthusiastic student studying cs @mcgill u/h2>

As a kid, I was told that I asked the questions "why?" and "how?" far too often. From a young age, I've been passionate about gaining a better understanding of how things function in the world around. I use this curiosity to find creative solutions to problems and gain knowledge in a variety of sectors.

When I'm not finding answers to the many questions which flood my mind, I work to create opportunites for women in STEM. Being in a highly competitive field can be overwhelming to anyone, and when people feel like the cards are stacked against them the oh so common imposter syndrome can start to creep up. Leading the McGill Women in Computer Science team for my second consecutive year now has been super rewarding as I've seen the difference our initiatives have made within our community.
